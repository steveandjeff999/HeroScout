Team Average Calculator

                    
                

                

                    
This tool allows you to look up a specific team's average performance metrics across all their matches.

                    
How to use:

                    
    
                        
  • Enter a team number in the field
    • 
                        
  • Select your preferred display format (JSON, Table, or List)
    • 
                        
  • Click the button to retrieve the team's average metrics
    • 
                    

                    
You can review specific game element metrics like auto scoring, teleop performance, and more to understand how the team typically performs.

                

                

                    
                

            

        

    

    

        

            

                

                    
All Team Averages

                    
                

                

                    
This section provides a comprehensive view of every team's average performance metrics at once.

                    
Key benefits:

                    
    
                        
  • Compare multiple teams simultaneously
    • 
                        
  • Identify top performers for specific game elements
    • 
                        
  • Evaluate the overall strength of the competition
    • 
                        
  • Find potential alliance partners based on complementary strengths
    • 
                    

                    
Use the different display formats to organize the data in the way that makes most sense for your analysis.

                

                

                    
                

            

        

    

    

        

            

                

                    
Team Rankings

                    
                

                

                    
The Team Rankings section shows all teams sorted by their total point contributions across all matches.

                    
Sorting options:

                    
    
                        
  • Points (Highest First) - See the top-scoring teams
    • 
                        
  • Points (Lowest First) - Identify teams that may need assistance
    • 
                        
  • Team Number (Ascending/Descending) - Find specific teams quickly
    • 
                    

                    
This information is valuable for alliance selection strategies, helping you choose high-performing partners or identify underperforming teams that might be avoided.

                

                

                    
                

            

        

    

    

        

            

                

                    
Match Data

                    
                

                

                    
This tool allows you to access detailed match-by-match performance data for any team.

                    
What you'll see:

                    
    
                        
  • Match-specific scoring details
    • 
                        
  • Auto and teleop performance for each match
    • 
                        
  • Performance trends across the competition
    • 
                        
  • Consistency indicators and outlier performances
    • 
                    

                    
Use this data to understand how a team's performance varies from match to match, identify their improvement patterns, and predict their future performance more accurately.

                

                

                    
                

            

        

    

    

        

            

                

                    
Times Broke

                    
                

                

                    
The Times Broke section tracks robot reliability by showing which teams have experienced breakdowns or malfunctions during matches.

                    
Why this matters:

                    
    
                        
  • Reliability is crucial for consistent alliance performance
    • 
                        
  • Teams with frequent breakdowns may be risky partners
    • 
                        
  • Identifying patterns in robot failures helps with strategic planning
    • 
                        
  • Can reveal teams that might perform better with technical assistance
    • 
                    

                    
Sort the data by frequency of breakdowns or team number to quickly find the information most relevant to your alliance selection process.

                

                

                    
                

            

        

    

    

        

            

                

                    
Times Tipped

                    
                

                

                    
The Times Tipped section tracks how frequently robots have lost balance and tipped over during matches.

                    
Why this matters:

                    
    
                        
  • Robot stability is important for consistent performance
    • 
                        
  • Teams that tip frequently may have balance or center-of-gravity issues
    • 
                        
  • Tipping can result in lost match time and reduced scoring
    • 
                        
  • May indicate design flaws that need to be addressed
    • 
                    

                    
Sort the data by frequency of tipping incidents or team number to quickly identify teams with potential stability concerns.

                

                

                    
                

            

        

    

    

        

            

                

                    
Match Estimation

                    
                

                

                    
This powerful tool predicts match outcomes by calculating expected points for specific alliance combinations.

                    
How it works:

                    
    
                        
  • Enter three team numbers for each alliance
    • 
                        
  • The system uses each team's average performance metrics
    • 
                        
  • Points are calculated based on game-specific scoring rules
    • 
                        
  • Results show predicted total points and breakdowns by team/category
    • 
                    

                    
Use this feature for alliance selection planning, match strategy development, and to identify potentially close matches where specific strategies might make the difference.

                

                

                    
                

            

        

    


    

        

            

                

                    
Charts

                    
                

                

                    
The Charts section provides visual comparisons of team performance across different metrics and match data.

                    
Available options:

                    
    
                        
  • Chart Types: Bar, Line, Radar, and Pie charts for different visualization needs
    • 
                        
  • Metrics: Total Score, Auto Score, Teleop Score, and Broke Rate
    • 
                        
  • Data Grouping: Match-by-match performance or team averages
    • 
                        
  • Team Selection: Add specific teams or all teams for comparison
    • 
                    

                    
Visual data makes it easier to identify patterns, strengths, and weaknesses that might not be immediately obvious in numeric data, helping you make more informed strategic decisions.

                

                

                    
                

            

        

    


    

        

            

                

                    
Compare Teams

                    
                

                

                    
This feature allows direct side-by-side comparison of up to three teams' performance metrics.

                    
Key features:

                    
    
                        
  • Color-coded comparison highlighting best and worst values
    • 
                        
  • All relevant performance metrics displayed simultaneously
    • 
                        
  • Choose between table and JSON display formats
    • 
                        
  • Easily identify complementary teams for alliance building
    • 
                    

                    
Team comparison is particularly useful during alliance selection to quickly evaluate potential partners and create a balanced alliance with complementary strengths.
